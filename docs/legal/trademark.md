## Why this isn't blocking

The BSL Licensor designation in `LICENSE` is "Twinlite Services Limited" —
the corporate entity, not the trademark. The trademark filing protects the
**name** "Vargate" against third-party use; it doesn't affect copyright
ownership of the code, which belongs to Twinlite Services Limited regardless
of trademark status.

If for any reason the trademark application is refused (e.g., a prior
registration we missed), the licensing posture does not change. We'd revisit
naming separately, but the Telemetry codebase remains BSL-licensed and owned
by Twinlite Services Limited.
